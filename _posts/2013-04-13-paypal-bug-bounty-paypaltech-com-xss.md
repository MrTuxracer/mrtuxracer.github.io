Great news! Today I received the second payment for another valid Cross-Site Scripting vulnerability covered by [PayPal's bug bounty](https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/security/reporting-security-issues) program.  This time the domain www.paypaltech.com was affected, which provides scripts and samples used for Instant Payment Notifications (IPNs). **Sometimes** ... being on the ethical side of hacking feels good ...  :-)
